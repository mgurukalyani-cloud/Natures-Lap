Major Rivers of India 🌊

India has one of the largest river systems in the world. Major rivers such as the Ganga, Yamuna, Brahmaputra, Godavari and Krishna support agriculture, transportation, drinking water supply and biodiversity across villages and cities.

Importance of Rivers

  • Irrigation for agriculture
  • Hydroelectric power generation
  • Support for rural livelihoods
  • Cultural and religious significance
  • Maintenance of ecological balance
